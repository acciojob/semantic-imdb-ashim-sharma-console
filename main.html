Summary

"Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone" follows the story of a young boy, Harry, who discovers he is a wizard on his eleventh birthday. He attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, makes friends and enemies, and uncovers the truth about his parents' mysterious deaths. Together with his friends, Harry faces many challenges and must protect the Sorcerer's Stone from falling into the hands of the dark wizard, Voldemort.
